About Us

Facing the beachfront, Vila Era offers 3-star accommodation in Sarande and has a garden, terrace and restaurant. With a bar, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. Guests can have a drink at the snack bar. Rooms have a flat-screen TV, and some rooms at the hotel have a balcony.

Guests at Vila Era can enjoy a continental breakfast. Sarande Main Beach is a few steps from the accommodation, while Saranda City Beach is 1.2 km from the property. The nearest airport is Ioannina Airport, 98 km from Vila Era Beach.
