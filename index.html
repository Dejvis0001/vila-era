A Few About Us

Facing the beachfront, Vila Era offers 3-star accommodation in Sarande and has a garden, terrace and restaurant. With a bar, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

Rooms have a flat-screen TV, and some rooms at the hotel have a balcony. Guests at Vila Era Beach can enjoy a continental breakfast.
